# Amazon-Prime-Video-Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Amazon Prime Video Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, designed to analyze and visualize content distribution across genres, ratings, countries, and time.

The dashboard provides meaningful insights into the platform’s content library, helping users understand trends, patterns, and key metrics in a visually engaging way.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Titles:** 9,655  
- **Total Ratings Categories:** 25  
- **Total Genres:** 519  
- **Total Directors:** 5,771  
- **Time Span:** 1920 – 2021  

---

## 📈 Features & Insights

### 🔹 Content Distribution by Rating
- Most content falls under the **13+ category**, followed by **16+ and ALL**.
- Indicates a strong focus on **teen and general audience content**.

### 🔹 Genre Analysis
- **Drama** is the most dominant genre.
- Followed by **Comedy**, **Drama & Suspense**, and **Animation/Kids**.
- Shows a preference for **story-driven and entertainment-heavy content**.

### 🔹 Movies vs TV Shows
- **Movies dominate (~80%)** of the platform.
- TV Shows make up a smaller portion (~20%).
- Highlights Amazon Prime’s stronger focus on **movie content**.

### 🔹 Geographic Distribution
- Content is heavily concentrated in:
  - **United States**
  - **India**
- Indicates major production and consumption hubs.

### 🔹 Trends Over Time
- Significant growth in content after the **year 2000**.
- Rapid increase in recent years reflects:
  - Expansion of streaming services
  - Increased content production
